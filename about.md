## Background
I have research experiences with __first-principles electronic structure calculations__ and simulations. What I focused on past few years was the __applications of density-functional theory (DFT) calculations__, exploiting atomic theoretical surface characterization approaches -- which covers advanced thermodynamics, cutting-edge theoretical spectroscopy and microscopy -- to understand metals or oxides surface properties in the field of theoretical surface and interface science.

In practice, the following keywords will represent my research interests and experiences:
- __Metal oxidation__
- __Theoretical aspect of catalysis__
- __Polymorphic (polytypic) expression of metal oxide__
- __Theoretical scanning tunneling microscopic (STM) and x-ray photoelectron spectroscopic (XPS) simulations__

Also, I demonstrated strong organizing skills, especially from good writing and presentations skills to a generic audience via publishing the articles as the first and co-authors in peer-reviewed journals and participating domestic and international conferences to share the my works.
