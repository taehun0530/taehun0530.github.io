The understanding of structure−property relations can accelerate technological development e.g., in clean enegy technologies and advanced (opto)-electronics. I examine and predict how the structural properties affect the stablilties and opto-electronic properties of heavy elements transition metal oxide (tungsten, molybdeuim, tantalium oxides) and their impact to potential applications (publication #6).
